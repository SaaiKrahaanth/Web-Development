HTML Text Formatting Tags Example

The <b> tag is used to make text bold without adding any extra importance or meaning to the text.

The <strong> tag is used to make text bold and also adds semantic importance, indicating that the text is significant or emphasized.

The <i> tag is used to make text italic, purely for visual styling, without any emphasis or special meaning.

The <em> tag is used to make text italic, and it also adds semantic emphasis, suggesting that the text is stressed or emphasized in context.
